# Skill: Preserve Feedback

When feedback exists, write `01_inputs/exercise-feedback-record.md` before diagnosis. Preserve the student's original meaning, uncertainty, problem references, answer annotations, and self-described confusion.

Do not correct, interpret, diagnose, or normalize the feedback in `01_inputs` except enough to make it readable and traceable.

Student answer annotations often appear immediately after selected options; treat parenthetical notes after an option as applying to that option.
