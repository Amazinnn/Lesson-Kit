# Skill: Knowledge Inventory

Before final writing, list all knowledge that must be covered. Include definitions, symbols, formulas, theorems, laws, models, conditions, boundaries, variable meanings, derivation conclusions, process steps, representation conventions, complexity, code-level rules, and recognition cues when they affect understanding or answers.

Preserve textbook order at the chapter/section level, but reorganize locally for dependency and clarity.

Use a two-pass inventory for textbook/PDF work: draft inventory from headings and visible structure, extract body details, then update or confirm the final inventory.
