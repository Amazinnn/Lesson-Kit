# Skill: Subject Mathematics And Physics

For mathematics, cover definitions, symbols, formulas, theorems, criteria, equivalences, proof/derivation ideas, conditions, boundary cases, and recognition cues.

For physics, cover definitions, laws, principles, physical models, formulas, meanings of quantities, units, directions, signs, frames, coordinates, applicability conditions, derivation conclusions, classic cases, and failure modes. Use formulas and compact derivation chains where useful, but keep mathematics in service of physics.
