# Skill: Printable Blank Space

For printable exercise sets, record the blank-space policy in `03_plans/exercise-set-format-plan.md` and verify it in `04_checks/exercise-blank-space-check.md`.

Default: one-part problem at least 5 blank lines; two-part at least 6; three-part at least 7; each additional part adds at least 1. Long proof, calculation, drawing, circuit, or programming tasks may require more.
