# Gate: Lesson Export Gate

The lesson passes only if it is organized by knowledge structure, not missed problems or feedback wording; it preserves textbook order at the chapter/section level while reorganizing locally for clarity; it includes required definitions, formulas, models, conditions, boundaries, dependencies, and body details; examples are sparse and functional; practice has not flooded the lesson; and final prose does not expose process phrases or raw feedback.

| Failure | Return to | Forbidden repair |
|---|---|---|
| Lesson reads like a wrong-answer notebook or feedback log | `intermediate/lesson_loop/03_plans/lesson-structure-plan.md` | Merely deleting phrases like “according to feedback” from final prose |
| Textbook body details are missing or treated as optional | `intermediate/lesson_loop/02_analysis/textbook-body-detail-extraction.md` and `intermediate/lesson_loop/02_analysis/chapter-knowledge-inventory.md` | Adding a miscellaneous final summary without updating the knowledge inventory |
| Feedback pollution appears | `intermediate/lesson_loop/02_analysis/feedback-to-lesson-map.md` and `intermediate/lesson_loop/03_plans/lesson-integration-plan.md` | Rewording raw feedback as “common mistakes” without stable knowledge revision requirements |
| Practice floods the lesson | `intermediate/lesson_loop/03_plans/lesson-problem-set-decision.md` | Deleting only a few exercises while keeping the lesson organized as a question bank |

Pass only after the earliest broken layer is repaired and the final prose is regenerated or revised from that repaired layer.
